# 05_25宫格连续叙事提示词 (25-Shot Narrative Storyboard)

## 核心功能
外网最火的25宫格连续叙事提示词，包含通用版和50宫格精简版，可直接丢给AI生成连续分镜。

## 25-Shot Storyboard Prompt (English)
```
Create a 25-shot continuous narrative storyboard.
Follow this shot sequence strictly:

1. Wide establishing shot
2. Medium wide shot to introduce character
3. Close-up on character’s face
4. Over-the-shoulder shot looking at object
5. Extreme close-up on detail
6. Medium shot of character moving
7. Low-angle shot to show power
8. High-angle shot to show vulnerability
9. Dutch angle for tension
10. Tracking shot following movement
11. Two-shot for interaction
12. Close-up on hand action
13. Wide shot of environment change
14. Medium close-up of reaction
15. Extreme close-up on eyes
16. Dolly-in shot for emphasis
17. Whip pan to new subject
18. Overhead shot for layout
19. Side profile shot
20. Slow-motion highlight moment
21. Close-up on conflict point
22. Medium shot of turning point
23. Wide shot for scale
24. Close-up emotional ending
25. Final wide shot for closure

Style: cinematic, smooth continuity, logical storytelling, dynamic composition.
```

## 25 格连续分镜 (Chinese)
```
创作25格连续叙事分镜，严格按以下镜头逻辑：
1. 远景交代环境
2. 中远景引入角色
3. 面部特写
4. 过肩镜头看向目标
5. 极端特写细节
6. 角色移动中景
7. 低角度镜头强调力量
8. 高角度镜头表现弱势
9. 荷兰倾斜角制造紧张
10. 跟拍镜头跟随动作
11. 双人镜头展示互动
12. 手部动作特写
13. 环境变化全景
14. 中近景反应镜头
15. 眼部极端特写
16. 缓慢推进强调重点
17. 甩镜切换新目标
18. 俯拍展示布局
19. 侧面轮廓镜头
20. 慢动作高光时刻
21. 冲突点特写
22. 转折点中景
23. 大远景体现规模
24. 情感收尾特写
25. 最终全景结束

风格：电影感、流畅连贯、叙事清晰、构图专业。
```

## 50 宫格超长叙事 (50-Shot Full Story)
```
Create a 50-shot full story cinematic sequence.
Structure:
01–10: Setup & introduction
11–25: Rising action & conflict
26–40: Climax & battle
41–50: Resolution & ending

Use a variety of shots: wide, medium, close-up, over-the-shoulder, low angle, high angle, tracking, dolly, slow motion.
Maintain visual rhythm and story logic.
```
