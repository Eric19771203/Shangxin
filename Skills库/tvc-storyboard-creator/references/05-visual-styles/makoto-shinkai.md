# Makoto Shinkai Style Guide

## Overview

Makoto Shinkai is a Japanese anime filmmaker known as "the new Miyazaki" for his breathtaking visual style. His films feature photorealistic backgrounds, dramatic natural phenomena, and emotionally resonant storytelling. This style has influenced advertising globally.

---

## Visual Characteristics

### Backgrounds

**Photorealistic Environments**:
- Real-world locations rendered in detail
- Cityscapes and urban settings
- Natural landscapes
- Weather effects

**Signature Elements**:
- Dramatic clouds
- Star-filled skies
- Sunrise/sunset
- Rain effects
- Light rays

### Color Palette

**Primary Tones**:
- Deep blues (#1a237e, #0d47a1)
- Warm oranges (#ff6f00, #ff8f00)
- Golden yellows (#ffb300)
- Soft whites (#f5f5f5)
- Pink/sakura (#f8bbd9)

**Lighting**:
- Golden hour warmth
- Blue hour coolness
- Dramatic contrast
- Volumetric light

### Sky Effects

**Cloud Formations**:
- Cumulus dramatic buildup
- Horizontal cloud layers
- Sunset color gradients
- Star-filled nights

**Atmosphere**:
- Haze/mist
- Rain streaks
- Light rays (crepuscular rays)
- Lens flare

---

## Composition Techniques

### Frame Dynamics

| Technique | Description | Usage |
|-----------|-------------|-------|
| Wide establishing | Full scene view | Setting mood |
| Depth layers | Foreground, mid, back | Emotional distance |
| Horizon placement | Low/high horizon | Drama/expansion |
| Window frames | Natural framing | Focus subject |

### Subject Treatment

**Human Characters**:
- Anime style bodies
- Emotional expressions
- Simple, relatable
- Hair detail

**Product Integration**:
- Natural placement
- Glowing/ethereal
- Light interaction
- Scale emphasis

---

## Lighting Characteristics

### Golden Hour

- Warm orange tones
- Long shadows
- Soft contrast
- Backlight effects

### Blue Hour

- Cool blue tones
- Mysterious atmosphere
- City lights appearing
- Peaceful mood

### Night Scenes

- City lights as stars
- Light trails
- Window lights
- Neon reflections

## Application to TVC

### Best For

- Tech products (phones, headphones)
- Youth brands
- Emotional storytelling
- Travel products
- Romance products
- Beauty/skincare
- Aspirational brands

### Scene Examples

| Scene | Shinkai Approach |
|-------|------------------|
| Product intro | Dramatic sky background |
| Emotional moment | Backlight, lens flare |
| Urban setting | Rain, neon, atmosphere |
| Nature scene | Flowers, weather effects |

### Duration Considerations

- **15s**: Dramatic establishing + close
- **30s**: Full emotional arc possible
- **45s+**: Multiple atmospheric moments

---

## Common Mistakes

### Avoid

1. **Flat backgrounds**: Must be detailed
2. **No sky**: Sky is signature element
3. **Harsh lighting**: Soft/golden required
4. **Realistic humans**: Anime style
5. **No atmosphere**: Weather adds emotion

---

## Mixing Styles

### Compatible Combinations

- **+ Disney**: Whimsical anime magic
- **+ Cyberpunk**: Rainy neon anime city
- **+ Traditional Chinese**: Japanese-Chinese fusion

### Challenging Combinations

- **+ Wes Anderson**: Too different moods
- **+ Gritty realism**: Style conflict
