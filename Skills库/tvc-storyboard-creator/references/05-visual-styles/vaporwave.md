# Vaporwave Style Guide

## Overview

Vaporwave is a retro-futuristic aesthetic mixing 80s/90s nostalgia with digital surrealism. Pink/purple gradients, glitch effects, and 80s nostalgia.

---

## Visual Characteristics

### Color Palette

**Primary**:
- Hot pink (#ff71ce)
- Electric purple (#01cdfe)
- Cyan (#05ffa1)

**Effects**:
- Gradients
- Glitch art
- Pixelation
- 80s elements
