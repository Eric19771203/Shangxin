# Cyberpunk Style Guide

## Overview

Cyberpunk is a dystopian future aesthetic combining advanced technology with societal decay. Characterized by neon lights, rain-soaked streets, and high-tech/low-life imagery.

---

## Visual Characteristics

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors**:
- Neon pink (#ff00ff)
- Cyan blue (#00ffff)
- Electric purple (#8b00ff)
- Hot orange (#ff4500)

**Atmosphere**:
- Dark backgrounds
- High contrast
- Light pollution
- Neon reflections

### Key Elements

- **Neon signs**: Japanese text, logos
- **Rain**: Wet surfaces, reflections
- **Technology**: Screens, holograms
- **Architecture**: Skyscrapers, decay
