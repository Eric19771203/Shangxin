# Scandinavian Minimalist Style Guide

## Overview

Scandinavian (Nordic) minimalism combines clean design with warmth (hygge). White spaces, natural light, and functional beauty define this aesthetic.

---

## Visual Characteristics

### Color Palette

**Primary**:
- Pure white (#ffffff)
- Soft grey (#f5f5f5)
- Light wood (#d4a373)
- Muted natural tones

**Accent**:
- Sage green (#8fbc8f)
- Dusty blue (#b0c4de)
- Terracotta (#cd5c5c)

### Key Elements

- White backgrounds
- Natural wood
- Plants Greenery
- Clean lines
- Natural light
- Minimal objects
