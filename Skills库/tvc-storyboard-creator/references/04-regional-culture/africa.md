# Africa Market Cultural Guide

## Overview

Africa is a diverse continent with 54 countries. Major markets include Nigeria, South Africa, Kenya, Egypt, and Ghana. This guide focuses on Sub-Saharan Africa as North Africa is covered in Middle East section.

---

## Common Mistakes

### Avoid

1. **Poverty stereotypes**: Never show as "helpless"
2. **Colonial framing**: Avoid outdated views
3. **Single story**: Show diversity
4. **Exoticizing**: Not "exotic others"
5. **Stereotypes**: Show as modern/progressive

---

## Representation Guidelines

### Do

- Show modern African cities
- Youth/diversity
- Professional settings
- Technology adoption
- Happy, aspirational
- Local celebrities
- Local languages
- Real scenarios

### Don't

- Safari/wildlife only
- Poverty/need
- Single story
- Outdated
- Tribal only
- "Dark continent"
