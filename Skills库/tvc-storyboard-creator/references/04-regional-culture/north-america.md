# North America Market Cultural Guide

## Overview

North America includes United States and Canada. The US is the largest single advertising market globally with distinct characteristics.

---

## Cultural Values

### United States

1. **Individualism**
   - Personal achievement
   - Self-expression
   - Personal success
   - Individual choice

2. **Innovation/Newness**
   - Latest/greatest
   - Tech-forward
   - Disruption valued
   - Future-focused

3. **Directness**
   - Straight talk
   - Clear benefits
   - Efficient communication
   - No-nonsense

4. **Humor**
   - Wit appreciated
   - Self-deprecation
   - Clever references
   - Entertainment value

5. **Aspiration**
   - Dream pursuit
   - Better future
   - Status achievement
   - Success stories

### Canada

- Similar to US with:
- More diversity emphasis
- Sustainability stronger
- Bilingual (English/French)
- Inclusivity focus

---

## Advertising Preferences

### Visual Style

**Loved Elements**:
- High contrast
- Bold colors
- Dynamic movement
- Clear focus
- Direct eye contact
- Celebrity presence
- Fast editing
- Professional production

**Composition**:
- Clear hierarchy
- Center-weighted
- Action-oriented
- Full frames

### Message Approach

**Effective**:
- Direct benefit
- Clear CTA
- Innovation focus
- Transformation stories
- Humor (when appropriate)
- Celebrity endorsement
- Social proof
- Urgency creation

**Less Effective**:
- Subtle messages
- Indirect communication
- Overly emotional (US)
- Class-based content
- Political topics
- Discrimination

## Creative Templates

### Template 1: Direct Benefit

**Best For**: Most products

**Structure**:
```
Opening: Problem/pain point
Setup: Solution introduction
Product: Feature/benefit
Payoff: Resolution
CTA: Act now
```

**Visual**:
- Clear before/after
- Direct presentation
- Bold graphics

### Template 2: Transformation

**Best For**: Beauty, fitness, personal care

**Structure**:
```
Opening: Starting point
Journey: Transformation
Product: Enabler
Result: New self
CTA: Start now
```

**Visual**:
- Dramatic before/after
- Emotional payoff
- Celebratory

### Template 3: Humor/Wit

**Best For**: Brands with personality

**Structure**:
Setup: Humorous scenario
Product: Witty resolution
Payoff: Clever CTA
```

**Visual**:
- Witty scenarios
- Self-deprecating
- Memorable

## Common Mistakes

### Avoid

1. **Discrimination**: Any form
2. **Political topics**: Stay neutral
3. **Class-based content**: Avoid poverty
4. **Wrong representation**: Diversity matters
5. **Stereotypes**: Avoid offensive
