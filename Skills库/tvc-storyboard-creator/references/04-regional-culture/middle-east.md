# Middle East Market Cultural Guide

## Overview

The Middle East region (MENA - Middle East and North Africa) represents a growing e-commerce market with unique cultural requirements. Key markets include Saudi Arabia, UAE, Egypt, and other Gulf states.

---

## Cultural Values

### Core Values

1. **العائلة (Family)**
   - Extended family structure
   - Family decision-making
   - Respect for elders
   - Family gatherings important

2. **الدين (Religion/Islam)**
   - Islamic principles guide life
   - Halal considerations
   - Ramadan observance
   - Religious celebrations important

3. **التقليد (Tradition)**
   - Cultural heritage important
   - Traditional values
   - Respect for customs
   - Intergenerational connections

4. **الكرامة (Dignity/Honor)**
   - Family honor important
   - Reputation matters
   - Social standing
   - Respect in community

5. **الكمال (Excellence/Perfection)**
   - Quality over quantity
   - Premium appreciated
   - Attention to detail
   - Best for family

## Advertising Preferences

### Visual Style

**Loved Elements**:
- Warm color tones (gold, amber, terracotta)
- Family scenes
- Traditional and modern blend
- Elegant, refined aesthetics
- High production value
- Religious/cultural symbols (when appropriate)
- Generational scenes
- Upscale settings

**Composition**:
- Group compositions (family)
- Centered, balanced
- Full, rich frames
- Quality focus

### Message Approach

**Effective**:
- Family-centric narratives
- Quality and premium focus
- Gift-giving culture
- Traditional values respected
- Long-term trust
- Religious appropriateness
- Generational connection
- Status and prestige

**Less Effective**:
- Alcohol imagery
- Pork/non-halal products
- Revealing clothing
- Religious symbols (unless Islamic)
- Western stereotypes
- Women in inappropriate contexts

## Religious Considerations

### Ramadan

**Most Important Advertising Period**:
- Digital spend peaks
- Changed daily rhythm
- Family focus intensifies
- Charitable themes appreciated

**Ramadan Creative**:
- Iftar moments
- Family gatherings
- Reflection themes
- Giving/charity
- Night scenes
- Warm lighting

**Timing Considerations**:
- Before iftar: Active purchasing
- After iftar: High engagement
- Avoid: Dawn scenes (suhoor time)

### Halal Requirements

**Must Avoid**:
- Pork in any form
- Alcohol
- Non-halal meat
- Religious symbols (Christian, Hindu, etc.)

**Products to Flag**:
- Food: Halal certification
- Cosmetics: Alcohol-free options
- Personal care: Ingredient review

## Common Pitfalls

### Critical Avoidances

1. **Alcohol**: Never show
2. **Pork**: Never reference
3. **Revealing clothing**: Modest presentation
4. **Wrong religious symbols**: Avoid Christian/Hindu imagery
5. **Women in inappropriate contexts**: Respectful portrayal
6. **Pets**: May be offensive in some contexts

### Sensitivity Points

1. **Direction**: Prayer direction awareness
2. **Language**: Right-to-left reading
3. **Gender**: Appropriate male/female interactions
4. **Royalty**: Respect for leaders
5. **Political**: Avoid
