## Regional Category Variations

### China Market Priorities
- **Top Categories**: Skincare, 3C, Fashion, Food
- **Focus**: Safety, quality, prestige, celebrity endorsement
- **Creative**: Large scale, emotional, aspirational

### Japan Market Priorities
- **Top Categories**: 3C, Beauty, Home
- **Focus**: Quality, detail, craftsmanship
- **Creative**: Minimalist, authentic, soft-sell

### US Market Priorities
- **Top Categories**: Beauty, Fashion, 3C
- **Focus**: Results, value, direct benefits
- **Creative**: Transformation, humor, celebrity

### Middle East Market Priorities
- **Top Categories**: Fashion, Beauty, Gold/Jewelry
- **Focus**: Family, luxury, tradition
- **Creative**: Family-centric, glamorous, Ramadan themes

### India Market Priorities
- **Top Categories**: Fashion, Beauty, Mobile
- **Focus**: Value, trend, aspirational
- **Creative**: Color, emotion, festivals

## Creative Approach Templates

### For High-Involvement Products (3C, Beauty, Fashion)
1. **Transformation Story**
   - Problem → Solution → Transformation → Payoff

2. **Lifestyle Aspiration**
   - Show ideal scenario
   - Product integration
   - Emotional benefit

3. **Feature Demonstration**
   - Highlight key feature
   - Show in action
   - Evidence/proof

### For Low-Involvement Products (Snacks, Basic Items)
1. **ASMR/Sensory**
   - Close-up details
   - Sound design
   - Immediate appeal

2. **Social Proof**
   - User moments
   - Sharing scenarios
   - Trend focus

### For Premium Products
1. **Craftsmanship Story**
   - Quality details
   - Heritage/craft
   - Exclusivity

2. **Aspirational Lifestyle**
   - Premium场景
   - Status signaling
   - Excellence focus
