# Eastern Europe & Russia Market Cultural Guide

## Overview

This region includes Russia, Poland, Ukraine, Czech Republic, Hungary, Romania, and other markets. Each has unique characteristics but shares some Soviet-era heritage.

---

## Cultural Values

### Core Values

1. **Heritage/History**
   - Rich cultural history
   - National pride
   - Tradition respected
   - Historical references

2. **Family**
   - Strong family bonds
   - Multi-generational
   - Family gatherings
   - Elderly respect

3. **Quality**
   - Quality over quantity
   - Value durability
   - Trust in established
   - Reliability matters

4. **Achievement**
   - Success orientation
   - Education valued
   - Professional status
   - Aspiration

5. **Art/Culture**
   - Appreciation for arts
   - Literary tradition
   - Music/ballet
   - Cultural sophistication

**Effective**:

## Common Mistakes

### Avoid

1. **Political sensitivity**: Russia especially
2. **Outdated stereotypes**: Modern markets
3. **Wrong language**: Many languages
4. **Quality issues**: Quality expected
5. **Over-simplification**: Diverse markets
