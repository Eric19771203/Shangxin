# China Market Cultural Guide

## Overview

China is one of the largest e-commerce markets globally, with distinct cultural values that significantly impact advertising effectiveness.

---

## Cultural Values

### Core Values

1. **家庭 (Family)**
   - Family cohesion is paramount
   - Respect for elders
   - Family gatherings and celebrations
   - Multi-generational households common

2. **面子 (Face/Mianzi)**
   - Social status and reputation
   - Showing respect publicly
   - Gifting culture
   - Social standing matters

3. **传统与现代融合 (Tradition Meets Modern)**
   - Respect for heritage
   - Aspiration to modern lifestyle
   - Blend of Eastern and Western aesthetics
   - Innovation with cultural roots

4. **团圆 (Reunion)**
   - Important festivals (Chinese New Year, Mid-Autumn)
   - Coming together of family
   - Symbolism in gifts

5. **吉祥 (Auspiciousness)**
   - Lucky numbers, colors, symbols
   - Red = luck, prosperity
   - Gold = wealth
   - Avoid: white, black for celebrations

## Advertising Preferences

### Visual Style

**Loved Elements**:
- Red and gold color accents
- Large scale productions
- Crowd/group scenes (showing popularity)
- Upscale settings
- Celebrity/influencer presence
- Dynamic editing
- High production value
- Bright, vibrant colors

**Composition**:
- Symmetry when showing quality
- Full-frame product shots
- Grand scale
- Clear hierarchy

### Message Approach

**Effective**:
- Direct benefit statements
- Celebrity endorsement
- Social proof (popularity)
- Status/luxury signaling
- Family-centric narratives
- Quality/precision focus
- Technology innovation

**Less Effective**:
- Subtle/hidden messages
- Minimalist without context
- Humor may not translate
- Individual over family

## Common Pitfalls to Avoid

### Cultural Mistakes

1. **Religious Imagery**
   - Avoid Buddhist/Christian imagery unless specifically appropriate
   - Check all symbolic elements

2. **Colors**
   - White/white flowers = death (avoid in celebrations)
   - Black in limited use
   - Green can mean "cuckold" (avoid in couples context)

3. **Numbers**
   - 4 = death (avoid)
   - 8 = prosperity (favored)
   - 7 = togetherness (favored)

4. **Language**
   - Avoid puns with negative meanings
   - Test translations thoroughly
   - Regional dialect considerations

5. **Model Selection**
   - Ensure diversity by region sold
   - Consider mainland vs. Taiwanese vs. Hong Kong preferences
   - Celebrity endorsements require careful selection

## TVC Length Considerations

### Platform Preferences

| Platform | Preferred Length | Format |
|----------|-----------------|--------|
| TV (CCTV) | 30s, 60s | Full production |
| Online Video | 15s, 30s | Various |
| Social (WeChat/Weibo) | 15s, 6s | Vertical |

### Story Density

- 30s TV: 8-12 shots recommended
- Social 15s: 4-6 shots
- Keep message simple

## Regional Variations

### First-Tier Cities (Beijing, Shanghai, Guangzhou, Shenzhen)
- More sophisticated
- Sustainability awareness
- Premium positioning
- International aesthetics

### Second and Third-Tier Cities
- Value-conscious
- Family-focused
- Status signaling
- Practical benefits

### Regional Preferences
- North: More direct, winter-focused
- South: Lighter, seafood/fresh focus
- West: Family/tradition stronger
