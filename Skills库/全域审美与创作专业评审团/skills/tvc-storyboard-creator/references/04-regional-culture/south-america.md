# South America Market Cultural Guide

## Overview

South America includes Brazil, Mexico, Argentina, Colombia, Peru, Chile, and other markets. Brazil is the largest economy and market.

---

## Cultural Values

### Core Values

1. **Familia (Family)**
   - Central to everything
   - Extended family
   - Family gatherings
   - Intergenerational

2. **Passion/Emotion**
   - Expressive culture
   - Warm emotions
   - Feeling-driven
   - Enthusiasm

3. **Celebration/Fiesta**
   - Festival culture
   - Joy and fun
   - Social gathering
   - Life enjoyment

4. **Personal Connection**
   - Relationships matter
   - Trust-based
   - Warm interactions
   - Community

5. **Hospitality**
   - Guest warmth
   - Generosity
   - Welcome culture
   - Sharing

---

## Market-Specific Notes

### Brazil

**Characteristics**:
- Largest market
- Portuguese language
- Carnival culture
- Soccer passion
- Beach culture

**Advertising**:
- Warm, emotional
- Family and fun
- Humor appreciated
- Vibrant visuals

### Mexico

**Characteristics**:
- Spanish language
- Strong family values
- Tradition importance
- Day of the Dead
- Celebrations

**Advertising**:
- Family-centric
- Traditional values
- Colorful
- Emotional warmth

### Argentina

**Characteristics**:
- Italian influence
- Beef/culture
- Tango passion
- European feel

**Advertising**:
- Sophisticated
- Quality focus
- European-Latin blend

### Colombia

**Characteristics**:
- Coffee culture
- Warm people
- Music/dance
- Family focus

**Advertising**:
- Warmth
- Family
- Natural beauty

## Advertising Preferences

### Visual Style

**Loved Elements**:
- Warm colors
- Bright, saturated
- People-focused
- Family/friends scenes
- Celebration moments
- Natural beauty
- Music/dance
- Full of life

**Composition**:
- People in frame
- Warm tones
- Full, rich
- Dynamic

### Message Approach

**Effective**:
- Emotional storytelling
- Family focus
- Warmth and joy
- Personal connection
- Celebration
- Humor (light)
- Social proof
- Fun

**Less Effective**:
- Cold/distant
- Too formal
- Serious only
- Negative messaging
- Poverty stereotypes

- People laughing

## Common Mistakes

### Avoid

1. **Poverty stereotypes**: Show as aspirational
2. **Regional insensitivity**: Each country different
3. **Wrong language**: Spanish vs Portuguese
4. **Inappropriate humor**: Cultural sensitivity
5. **Overly serious**: Warmth expected
