# Disney Animation Style Guide

## Overview

Disney animation represents timeless visual storytelling with vibrant colors, expressive characters, and magical atmospheres. This style evokes nostalgia while maintaining modern appeal.

---

## Visual Characteristics

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors**:
- Vibrant primaries (red, blue, yellow)
- Rich jewel tones
- Warm gradients
- Saturated saturation

**Color Coding**:
- Hero's journey: Warm → Cool → Warm
- Villain: Dark, desaturated
- Magic: Sparkles, glows
- Nature: Greens, blues

### Character Design

**Proportions**:
- Big eyes (emotion)
- Expressive faces
- Simplified bodies
- Exaggerated features

**Expressions**:
- Clear emotions
- Readable feelings
- Cartoon physics
- Appeal principles

### Animation Quality

**Movement**:
- Smooth in-betweens
- Anticipation/follow-through
- Squash and stretch
- Exaggeration

**Effects**:
- Sparkles/magic dust
- Light glows
- Particle effects
- Smooth gradients

---

## Composition

### Framing

| Technique | Description | Effect |
|-----------|-------------|--------|
| Full frame | Character fills frame | Intimacy |
| Wide shot | Environment visible | Scope |
| Over shoulder | Dialogue scenes | Connection |
| Low angle | Heroic | Power |

### Visual Hierarchy

1. Main character
2. Supporting elements
3. Background
4. Atmosphere

---

## Lighting

### Key Characteristics

1. **Warm, Even Lighting**
   - Pleasant mood
   - Inviting atmosphere

2. **Rim Lighting**
   - Character separation
   - Magical glow

3. **Source Lighting**
   - Clear light direction
   - Shadow casting

4. **Magical Light**
   - God rays
   - Sparkles
   - Glow effects

## Application to TVC

### Best For

- Family products
- Kids/baby items
- Toys
- Aspirational brands
- Magical transformations
- Heartwarming stories
- Brand nostalgia

### Scene Examples

| Scene | Disney Approach |
|-------|-----------------|
| Product hero | Character qualities, glow |
| Transformation | Sparkle effects |
| Family moment | Heartwarming, warm |
| Magical moment | Light rays, sparkles |

### Duration Considerations

- **15s**: Quick magical moment
- **30s**: Mini story arc
- **45s+**: Full transformation

---

## Common Mistakes

### Avoid

1. **Flat colors**: Needs gradients
2. **No magic**: Add sparkle/glow
3. **Serious only**: Needs warmth
4. **Cluttered**: Clean composition
5. **Realistic**: Stylized approach

---

## Mixing Styles

### Compatible Combinations

- **+ Traditional Chinese**: Disney meets Asia
- **+ Vintage**: Nostalgic magic
- **+ Minimalist**: Clean magical

### Challenging Combinations

- **+ Gritty**: Complete contrast
- **+ Cyberpunk**: Tone clash
