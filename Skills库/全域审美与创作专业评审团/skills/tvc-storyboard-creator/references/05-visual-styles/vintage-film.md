# Vintage Film Style Guide

## Overview

Vintage film aesthetic evokes nostalgia through film grain, faded colors, and timeless atmosphere reminiscent of classic cinema.

---

## Visual Characteristics

### Color Palette

**Tones**:
- Faded warmth
- Sepia undertones
- Muted greens
- Desaturated reds

**Effects**:
- Film grain
- Light leaks
- Vignette
- Scratches
